# Dr. Jagdish Hiremath — Official Profile

## Biography

Dr. Jagdish Hiremath is India's renowned Interventional Cardiologist based in Pune. He has dedicated his career to introducing novel therapies and cutting-edge technologies to Pune's cardiac care landscape while promoting preventive cardiology awareness across urban and rural populations of Maharashtra and India.

## Medical Expertise & Procedures Offered

**Coronary Interventions**
Angioplasty of the coronary arteries — a minimally invasive procedure to open blocked or narrowed arteries and restore blood flow to the heart.

**Renal Artery Angioplasty**
Minimally invasive procedure to open narrowed or blocked arteries supplying the kidneys, improving blood flow and preventing kidney damage.

**Carotid Artery Angioplasty**
Procedure to widen narrowed carotid arteries, restoring blood flow to the brain and managing associated cardiovascular risk.

**Cardiac Imaging — Colour Doppler**
Advanced colour Doppler imaging for all age groups including neonates, for precise vascular and cardiac assessment.

**Balloon Valve Treatment**
Minimally invasive procedure to widen narrowed heart valves, improving blood flow and cardiac function.

**Device Closure for Congenital Defects**
Minimally invasive procedure to seal heart defects (e.g. holes in the heart) without open surgery.

**Pacemakers & Heart Failure Devices**
Implantation of devices to regulate cardiac rhythm and enhance heart function in patients with arrhythmia or heart failure.

**Cardiac Rehabilitation**
Comprehensive rehabilitation programmes designed to aid recovery, improve heart health, and enhance overall well-being post-procedure.
